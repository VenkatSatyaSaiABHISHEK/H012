Power Consumption (kWh) = Device Wattage √ó Hours Used √∑ 1000

Cost Calculation

Energy Cost = Power Consumption (kWh) √ó Electricity Rate (per kWh)

Savings Calculation

Money Saved = (Auto-Off Hours √ó Device Wattage √∑ 1000) √ó Electricity Rate
